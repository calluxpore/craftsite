Pottery Workshop

Organised by: Master Craftsman Rajesh Kumar
Date: December 15, 2024 - December 17, 2024
Location: Traditional Arts Center, Khurja, Uttar Pradesh

Immerse yourself in the ancient art of pottery making with traditional techniques passed down through generations. Learn to create beautiful ceramic pieces using authentic methods and local clay.

About the Event

Join us for an immersive 3-day pottery workshop where you'll discover the timeless art of ceramic creation. This hands-on experience will take you through the complete pottery-making process, from preparing clay to firing your finished pieces.

Under the guidance of Master Craftsman Rajesh Kumar, who has over 30 years of experience in traditional pottery, you'll create your own unique ceramic pieces. The workshop includes all materials, tools, and firing of your creations. You'll also learn about the rich history of Khurja pottery, famous throughout India for its distinctive blue pottery and ceramic artistry.
