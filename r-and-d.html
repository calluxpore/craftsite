Research & Development

Connecting students, researchers, and institutions with handicraft clusters, research funding, live projects, and internship opportunities worldwide.

Craft Cluster Visits

Explore itineraries and details for educational visits to handicraft clusters in India and abroad. Perfect for students and faculty from schools, colleges, and universities.

Japanese Textile Crafts

Discover traditional Japanese textile techniques including shibori (resist dyeing), katazome (stencil dyeing), Nishijin-ori brocades, tsumugi silks, boro and sashiko mending arts, Sakiori rag weaving, temari balls, and braided cords.

Japan
7-10 Days

Bamboo Crafts of Vietnam

Experience the ancient art of Vietnamese bamboo craft. Learn traditional techniques for creating functional and decorative items, from baskets and furniture to intricate sculptures, all rooted in sustainable practices.

Vietnam
5-7 Days

Wool Textile of North East India

Explore the rich wool textile traditions of North East India. Learn about traditional weaving techniques, patterns, and the cultural significance of wool textiles in the region.

North East India
4-6 Days
